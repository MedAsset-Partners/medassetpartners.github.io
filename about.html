About MedAsset Partners

Your trusted partner in medical equipment maintenance, ensuring your practice runs smoothly so you can focus on what matters most—your patients.

Our Story

Founded by medical equipment specialists with years of combined experience, MedAsset Partners was born from a simple observation: healthcare providers deserve better equipment maintenance and technology support.

We've seen too many practices struggle with unreliable equipment, expensive emergency repairs, and compliance headaches. That's why we created a comprehensive maintenance solution that keeps your equipment running optimally while you focus on delivering exceptional patient care.

From solo wellness clinics to multi-site medical groups, we've help practices maintain their equipment investments and improve patient experience. As a Christian-founded company, we operate with the understanding that our work extends beyond business success to encompass a calling to serve in the sacred work of healing and correction.
